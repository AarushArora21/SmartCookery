Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tbsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 cup Tomato Puree
  • 1/2 cup Cream
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 2 tbsp Oil

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, and garam masala. Let it sit for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and cook the chicken until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, melt butter, add tomato puree, and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the cooked chicken, cream, and salt. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  5. Serve hot with naan or rice.
