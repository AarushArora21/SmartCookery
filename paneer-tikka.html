Paneer Tikka

Ingredients

  • 250g Paneer (cottage cheese)
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Tikka Masala
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl to make the marinade.
  2. Cube the paneer and coat it with the marinade. Let it sit for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat a grill pan and cook the marinated paneer pieces until golden brown.
  4. Serve hot with green chutney and onion rings.
