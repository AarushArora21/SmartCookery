Chole Bhature

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 2 Onions, chopped
  • 2 Tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tbsp Chole Masala
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • For Bhature: 2 cups All-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp Yogurt
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • Water to knead
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Cook the chickpeas in a pressure cooker for 3-4 whistles until soft.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, saut√© onions, and ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Add tomatoes, red chili powder, chole masala, and cooked chickpeas. Let it simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. For Bhature, knead the dough with flour, yogurt, baking powder, salt, and water. Let it rest for 1 hour.
  5. Roll out the dough and fry bhatures in hot oil until puffed and golden.
  6. Serve the chole with freshly made bhature.
