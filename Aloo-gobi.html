Aloo Gobi

Ingredients

  • 3 medium-sized Potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 medium-sized Cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 2 Tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until soft, then add turmeric, red chili powder, and garam masala.
  4. Add potatoes and cauliflower, stir well, cover, and cook for 20-25 minutes until tender.
  5. Serve hot with roti or rice.
